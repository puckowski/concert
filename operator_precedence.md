# Operator precedence
  
| Precedence | Operator | Description                 |
|:-----------|:---------|:----------------------------|
|1           |<<        |Bitwise left shift           |
|1           |>>        |Bitwise right shift          |
|2           |*         |Multiplication               |
|2           |/         |Division                     |
|2           |%         |Remainder                    |
|3           |+         |Addition                     |
|3           |-         |Subtraction                  |
|4           ||         |Bitwise OR                   |
|4           |&         |Bitwise AND                  |
|4           |^         |Bitwise XOR                  |
